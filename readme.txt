**long-term-project** (in progress)
motionOS is a hands-free PC control system that enables users
to control their computer entirely through natural body movements
— hand gestures and eye gaze — using only a standard webcam.
No special hardware, no controllers, no physical contact required.

The system begins as a gesture-controlled interface and evolves
over four years into a full platform with eye gaze tracking,
a web dashboard, AI-powered recognition, and database-backed
user profiles.


PURPOSE
**Accessibility**
Designed with motor-disabled users in mind — people with
conditions such as ALS, paralysis, or limited motor control
who cannot use traditional input devices. GazeGest provides
an alternative that requires only natural body movement.

**Medical Use**
Enables touchless screen interaction in sterile environments
such as operation theatres, diagnostic labs, and rehabilitation
centers where physical contact with devices is not practical.

**AI Integration & Skill Development**
Built to apply and deepen practical knowledge of computer
vision and AI integration — moving beyond academic learning
